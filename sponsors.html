Sponsors


Sponsors are crucial members of the HKSN community. Not only do sponsors provide financial support for the club, they assist the club with hosting events from providing facilities to making food and drinks. In exchange, HKSN provides marketing to the McGill student body and allows sponsors to reach a large and active community of students and alumni.

HKSN provides free advertising for all sponsors, in the form of features on the listserv, Instagram posts, and more reaching over 3500 members around the world. If you would like to become a sponsor, have a look at our sponsorship package, and contact us.
